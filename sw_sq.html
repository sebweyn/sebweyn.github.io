Welcome to my personal page!

Here you can find and download copies of my publications, read occasional posts on topics I find interesting, and get in touch.

I'm a graduate student in the laboratory of Chaolin Zhang. We are members of the Department of Systems Biology and the Motor Neuron Center at the Columbia University Medical Center. I explore basic biology using computational tools with a current focus on the regulation of RNA splicing. Outside the lab, I'm also interested in technology and music.
